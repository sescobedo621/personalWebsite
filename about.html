About Shelby

Media is Shelby's speciality. She attended Point Loma Nazarene University in beautiful San Diego, CA, where she studied Media Communications. While going to school, she worked in the college's television studio as a Technical Assistant and created several videos for non-profit organizations. After graduating with honors, Shelby packed her bags and moved to Casper, WY, to work as a Production Assistant for KTWO, the local ABC news station. She also worked as a master control operator on weekends. After spending a year as a Cowboy, Shelby became a Cornhusker, and worked at NTV, an ABC news affiliate in Kearney, NE. Within her two years in the television news business, Shelby noticed the media industry was going toward websites and applications. Instead of being left behind, she decided to follow the trend and attended a coding bootcamp in Greenwood Village, CO to become a developer.

Shelby grew up in the suburbs of Denver, CO. When she isn't practicing her programming skills, Shelby enjoys hiking in the beautiful Rocky Mountains, watching and analyzing movies, and cheering on her beloved Broncos and Rockies.
